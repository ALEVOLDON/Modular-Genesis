## Theory

An audiovisual system becomes coherent when the visual layer responds to actual musical structure.

Useful inputs include:

- amplitude
- onset intensity
- frequency bands
- rhythmic density

## Why It Matters

Reactive visuals should not be random decoration. They should reveal musical behavior.

## Mapping Examples

- bass energy -> scale or mass
- transient activity -> flashes or particle bursts
- drone intensity -> background motion

## Practice

Choose one musical feature and one visual parameter, then define the mapping clearly in writing.
