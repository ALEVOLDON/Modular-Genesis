## Theory

A generative patch becomes easier to manage when voices are separated.

Instead of printing one stereo master, you can isolate:

- kick
- bass
- melody
- texture
- drone

## Why It Matters

This gives the DAW stronger control over:

- compression
- EQ
- arrangement
- automation

## Patch Idea

```text
Voice 1 -> Output 1
Voice 2 -> Output 2
Voice 3 -> Output 3
Voice 4 -> Output 4
```

## Practice

Design a four-voice routing map and define what each output should carry.
