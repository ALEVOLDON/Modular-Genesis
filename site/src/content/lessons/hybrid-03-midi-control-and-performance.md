## Theory

Hybrid does not only mean audio routing. It also means control routing.

MIDI can influence:

- pitch
- triggers
- macro modulation
- performance gestures

## Why It Matters

A self-running system becomes much more useful when a performer can intervene:

- open the filter
- change density
- transpose a sequence
- trigger structural transitions

## Patch Idea

```text
MIDI controller -> Rack MIDI module -> pitch / gates / mapped macros
```

## Practice

Choose three parameters in your patch that should remain performable by hand.
